Usability Study

Method

    I studied Rooms, The target audience is creators of any age. I tasked my subject with Creating a room, Searching on the site, and Interacting with a room.

Results

    My subject was able to complete the tasks. It would have been much easier if he hadnt gotten distracted. It took a 40 minutes to finish the first task because he got disteracted. the other two it took abouut 4-6 steps to complete.

Discussion

    I learned this website is very attention grabbing, the current design is quite easy to use, because its just a few buttons and a search bar. I dont think they could change the design anymore,
